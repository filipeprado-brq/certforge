<objective>
Port the BRQ quiz presentational components to TSX and build the mode-selection screen and the
question runner. The runner is where the engine (Plan 04-01) becomes interactive: it selects
questions via injected `Math.random`, ticks a real interval that feeds `Date.now()` into the pure
timer, reveals correctness+explanation in non-timed modes, defers all feedback in timed mode, and
auto-submits on expiry. On finish it calls `onFinish(gradeResult, questions, answers)` — the parent
(Plan 04-03) records the attempt and shows results.

Purpose: Deliver QUIZ-01 (answer→reveal+explanation) and QUIZ-02..05 (the four modes' selection +
feedback timing) as real UI built on the tested engine.
Output: `QuizParts.tsx` (ScoreDial/DomainBars/PassChip/Timer), `ModeSelect.tsx`, `QuizRunner.tsx`
(+ component tests), `quizModes.ts` (QUIZ_MODES copy).
</objective>
